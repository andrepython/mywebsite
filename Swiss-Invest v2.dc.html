FREQUENTLY ASKED
Are all exchanges with the lawyer confidential?+

Yes. All exchanges are covered by professional secrecy (attorney–client privilege) and handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with Swiss Bar rules and applicable law.

Can a dispute be resolved without going to court?+

Where appropriate, the firm can negotiate, mediate, or facilitate conciliation on your behalf — before, during, or instead of formal court proceedings.

Do I need an appointment for a legal consultation?+

Yes. All consultations are by appointment so the firm can review your situation in advance. Legal consultations are available Monday to Thursday, 16:30–18:00, at a fixed fee of CHF 30 for 30 minutes.

What investment opportunities does Swiss-Invest facilitate?+

Primarily transactions involving Swiss companies (acquisitions, sales, shareholdings), Swiss real estate (residential, commercial, development land), and alternative assets including fine art, collectible watches, jewellery, and private collections.

Are the investment opportunities publicly available?+

Some are publicly available, while a significant number originate from private networks and off-market transactions accessible exclusively through the firm's established network.

Do you work with international investors?+

Yes. Swiss-Invest assists both Swiss and international investors and clients seeking legal or investment advisory services in Switzerland.
